      Diet & Exercise

      ✅ Eat: Leafy greens, fruits, nuts, yogurt, dark chocolate.
      ❌ Avoid: Junk food, excess caffeine, too much sugar.
      💪 Exercises: Light yoga, stretching, walking, breathing exercises.

      Cycle Guide

      Your menstrual cycle usually lasts 28 days. Keep track of your flow, symptoms, and energy. This helps you know your body better.

      When to See a Doctor

      ⚠️ Severe pain not relieved by medicine.
      ⚠️ Extremely heavy bleeding.
      ⚠️ Missed periods for several months (not due to pregnancy).
      ⚠️ Dizziness or fainting during cycle.
